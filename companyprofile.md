## Company Profile

Orisco Energy Limited is an indigenous company providing a broad spectrum of services and expertise in the oil and gas industry, both locally and internationally.

As an indigenous company, our focus extends beyond oil and gas to support broader sectors, including power generation, infrastructure development, and other related areas that contribute to Nigeria’s industrial and economic growth.

Orisco Energy is committed to total client satisfaction, leveraging a highly efficient local and international procurement network to deliver optimal results. Our values are deeply rooted in building a strong foundation for a sustainable and promising future.

## Environmental Protection Policy

All operations are planned and executed to minimize environmental impact. Waste is managed responsibly, emissions are controlled, and contaminated materials are treated prior to authorized discharge.

Orisco Energy complies fully with environmental regulations of the Federal Republic of Nigeria, including FEPA and relevant local authorities.

We enforce proactive reporting and resolution of unsafe conditions to prevent incidents.
